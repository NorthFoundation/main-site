Our Mission

To provide high-quality computer-related education in the Midwestern United States.

Our Belief

North Foundation believes that knowledge and information should be open and accessible to everyone. We focus exclusively on providing education on internet technologies through organizing conferences, events, and other training events open to computer professionals, workers from a broad range of areas who work frequently with computer technology, and interested members of the general community.
